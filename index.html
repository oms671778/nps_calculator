What is NPS? - Basic Concepts

The National Pension Scheme (NPS) is a government-backed retirement planning program that helps you build a retirement corpus through regular contributions.

Key Features:

  • Regular Contributions: Invest monthly/annually until retirement
  • Market-Linked Returns: Your money is invested in equity and debt funds
  • Tax Benefits: Additional ₹50,000 deduction under Section 80CCD(1B)
  • Compounding Effect: Your returns generate more returns over time

How Withdrawal Works:

  • At retirement (minimum age 60), you must use 40% of corpus to buy annuity (pension)
  • You can withdraw 60% as tax-free lump sum
  • The annuity provides regular monthly pension for life

Why Start Early?

Starting at age 25 vs 35 can increase your retirement corpus by 50-100% due to compounding. Even small regular investments grow significantly over decades.

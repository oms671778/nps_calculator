Understanding the National Pension Scheme

  • Regular Contributions: Invest a fixed amount monthly until retirement. Consistency helps build wealth over time.
  • Power of Compounding: Your returns generate more returns. Starting 5 years earlier can increase your corpus by 40-50%.
  • Tax Benefits: Extra ₹50,000 tax deduction under Section 80CCD(1B) over the standard ₹1.5 lakh limit.
  • Flexible Investment Options: Choose between equity (up to 75%), corporate bonds, and government securities.
  • Market-Linked Returns: Higher potential returns than traditional fixed deposits, but with market risks.
  • Portability: Your NPS account stays with you even if you change jobs or cities.

Important Points to Remember:

1. NPS is a long-term retirement product with withdrawal restrictions until age 60

2. Returns are market-linked and not guaranteed

3. The calculator provides projections based on mathematical formulas

4. Actual returns may vary based on market conditions

About This Project

Project Timeline

Developed during the 2024-2025 academic year as part of the Engineering Mathematics curriculum under the Virtual Lab initiative.

Lab Type

Virtual Laboratory for Engineering Mathematics demonstrating practical applications of compound interest, exponential growth, and financial mathematics.

Collaboration

Inter-departmental collaboration between Applied Sciences & Humanities (mathematical concepts) and Information Technology (technical implementation).

Technologies Used

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla), Chart.js for visualizations, responsive web design principles.

Citation Guidelines

When referencing materials from this virtual lab in academic work, please use the following citation formats:

APA Format (Book):
Smith, J. R., & Johnson, M. K. (2022). Financial Mathematics for Pension Plans (3rd ed.). Academic Press.
APA Format (Journal Article):
Gupta, A., & Verma, P. (2023). Mathematical Modeling of Defined Contribution Pension Plans. Journal of Financial Engineering, 15(2), 45-67. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfe.2023.02.003
APA Format (Website):
Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority. (2023). PFRDA Annual Report 2022-23. Retrieved from https://www.pfrda.org.in
